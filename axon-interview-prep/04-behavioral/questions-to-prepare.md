# Behavioral Questions To Prepare

## Core Questions

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why Axon?
3. Why this role?
4. Tell me about a technical challenge you solved.
5. Tell me about a time you improved reliability.
6. Tell me about a time you made a mistake.
7. Tell me about a time you received feedback.
8. Tell me about a time you disagreed with a teammate.
9. Tell me about a time you had to learn quickly.
10. Tell me about a production issue you debugged.

## Stories To Reuse

- Ownership: Hasaki payment system or order/invoice migration.
- Technical challenge: 30M+ record migration with Debezium CDC and fallback jobs.
- Reliability improvement: optimistic locking, distributed locks, retries, and timeouts.
- Collaboration: payment service with ZaloPay, VNPAY, Techcombank, internal wallet.
- Debugging: OpenTelemetry tracing for logistics and marketplace integrations.
- Learning quickly: moving from DevOps internship to backend/cloud production work.
- Customer impact: reducing latency to under 100ms or improving issue detection by 40%.
