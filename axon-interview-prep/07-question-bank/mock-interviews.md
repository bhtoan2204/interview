2. Why Axon?

6. What are your salary expectations?

## Mock 2: Technical Screen

2. What is idempotency?

4. What is the difference between optimistic locking and distributed locking?

8. What questions do you have for me?

## Mock 3: Backend Deep Dive

5. Explain the 30M+ record migration.
6. How did Debezium CDC work in your system?

8. What would you do differently next time?

## Mock 4: System Design

Prompt:

> Design a notification service for public safety users.

Expected coverage:

- Requirements
- API
- Data model
- Queue
- Provider integrations
- Retry and DLQ
- User preferences
- Priority and rate limits
- Observability
- Security and privacy

Follow-up questions:

1. How do you avoid sending duplicate notifications?
2. How do you handle provider failure?
3. How do you support high-priority alerts?
4. How do you trace one notification across services?
5. How do you test this system?
